Why Choose Us?

We take pride in breeding only the best golden retriever puppies by the best AKC-registered golden retriever breeders. Read below to learn why your newest furry family member should be from one of Ohio’s top golden retriever breeders.

We Value Family

We love family. We love our kids. We love our puppies. We believe that no family is complete without a Golden Retriever. It’s the right way to grow up.

Our AKC Registered Golden Retriever Breeders Guarantee Our Pups

Be assured that we stand behind our AKC golden retriever puppies by offering a 2-year genetic health guarantee. Want to learn more about how we have the best golden retriever puppies? Read our Guarantee Page .

Raised With Lots of Love

We ensure our puppies are raised with lots of love. We strive to give them healthy, routine interactions with our own children. The best golden retriever puppies are socially well-adjusted, and we provide the groundwork for a long, happy, healthy relationship with your tail-wagging best friend.

Authenticity

With an appointment, we welcome you to come meet us, visit our premises, view the pedigrees, and see our lovely AKC golden retriever puppies. Our AKC-registered golden retriever breeders have nothing to hide, unlike a lot of golden retriever breeders that distribute through pet stores or do not allow you on their premises. Trust us. If golden retriever breeders won’t let you on the premises, there is usually a reason that is not in your best interest.

Certified Vet-Check

Have the peace of mind knowing our AKC golden retriever puppies have been thoroughly health-checked by a certified veterinarian.

Vaccination

For your convenience, full up-to-date vaccination and deworming records are provided, so that you and your veterinarian can continue proper preventative care of your precious pup.
