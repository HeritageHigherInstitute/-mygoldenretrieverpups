Introducing Chai!

I come from a line of true Goldens—calm, intelligent, and full of heart. I’ll be your constant, your comfort, and your joy, wrapped in the softest cream fur you’ve ever touched.

I have had Early Neurological Stimulation (ENS) training, which is a protocol used to stimulate my senses starting from the second day after I was born up until 16 days old! By gently engaging my senses, my brain goes into rapid development much sooner and I will be able to adapt to changes in environment more smoothly!

My sire Gunner is a poised, confident cream who is OFA Hip &Elbow (preliminary testing), Heart and Patella certified . My mother Oakley is a gentle medium Golden who is OFA Hip, Elbow, Heart and Patella certified . Both of my parents have been genetically health tested to be sure I am free of common genetic disease.
